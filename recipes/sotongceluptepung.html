Sotong celup tepung

Description

Sotong celup tepung (fried calamari; literally: flour-dipped squid) is a Malaysian seafood dish. This recipe is adapted from this page.

Ingredients

  • Squid
  • Wheat flour
  • Rice flour
  • Egg white
  • Curry powder
  • Salt
  • Vegetable oil

Steps

  1. Clean the squid and cut it into rings, drain and pat dry with kitchen paper towel.
  2. Add the squid into the egg white and mix them well. Meanwhile, in a large plastic bag, shake together the wheat flour, rice flour, curry powder and salt. Drain and add the squid into the flour mixture, and shake to coat them in it, Transfer the end product into the seive, and lightly shake off the excess flour mixture.
  3. Heat the oil in a medium pot, deep fry the squid until they turn golden brown. Once so, drain the squid and place it on the kitchen paper towel to absorb excess oil.
  4. Serve with dipping sauce or chilli sauce.
